Chicken Parmesan Recipe

Description

This classic italian dish is best served with a side of pasta. Whether it be shared with family and friends or eaten solo, you're guaranteed to be satisfied. Yum.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Marinara Sauch
  • Pasta of your choice
  • Paremsan Cheese
Steps
  1. Bread Chicken Breast
  2. Cook Chicken
  3. Add Mozzarella
  4. Pour Marinara
  5. Add Paremsan Cheese
  6. Serve with Pasta
