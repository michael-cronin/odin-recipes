Peanut Butter and Jelly Recipe

Description

A staple in any aspiring chef's repertoire, the peanut butter and jelly sandwich will satiate even a grizzly bear's hunger.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
Steps
  1. Spread Peanut Butter on one side of bread.
  2. Spread Jelly on otherside of bread.
  3. Combine piece of bread and cut to taste. (I prefer triangles)
